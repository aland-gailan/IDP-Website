About Us

Dedicated to advancing Iraq's economic landscape through local and international trade, investment, and strategic collaboration. IDP serves as a bridge between Iraqi enterprises and global partners, fostering partnerships that stimulate growth, strengthen infrastructure, and promote sustainable development. By facilitating connections and supporting economic initiatives, IDP plays a vital role in shaping a prosperous future for Iraq.

Mission

Our mission is to foster partnerships between local and international companies, creating a collaborative ecosystem that supports trade, drives innovation, and empowers economic development in Iraq. We are committed to advancing initiatives that contribute to infrastructure growth, sustainability, and long-term economic resilience.
